What we offer TO YOU

Recreational Classes

We have recreational classes for all ages and abilities running 7 days a week.

We pride ourselves on having the best recreational program in the country. We focus on confidence and coordination in a safe and fun environment.

We strive to make the time each child spends with us an enjoyable and learning experience.

Advanced Classes

As a club, we offer a competitive program for Artistic Gymnastics. Children will be selected from their class with us based on talent identification and testing criteria. We take part in National and international competitions and each year we are one of the most successful clubs in the country across all levels nationally. We are proud to announce that we have a number of boys and girls on National Squads for this year.
